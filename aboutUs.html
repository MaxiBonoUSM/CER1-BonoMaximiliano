About Us:

Maximiliano Bono

Founder and Current CEO

As everyone else, we started with a dream, a very big and ambitious dream. To one day give people the best quality and most affordable footwear that ever was in the market. It was a hard and arduous task but eventually we got where we are today, not with luck, but with hard work and passion.

Our current objective is to continue providing people with our amazingly good quality footwear and also expand our business to not only footwear, but also general clothing items! We are also currently in the process of arranging a collaborative effort with a known NGO to provide footwear to children in Africa and in low income communities around Central America, so stay tuned!
